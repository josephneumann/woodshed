    How to drill it

    Set the start note to where your bend begins (for the flagship — Slow Dancing in a Burning Room — a lot of it is full-step bends on the G and B strings around the 7th–10th frets). Play the start note clean, then push the bend up. When you reach the top and hold for a beat, the lab grades the peak against your target: within ±10 cents = Nailed, ±25 = Close, otherwise Off with the direction. It also reports how steady the hold was (lower cents of wobble = steadier) and how long the bend took to reach pitch. Switch to Vibrato mode and sustain near the start note to read your rate and width live.
