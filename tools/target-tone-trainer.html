Target-Tone Trainer

Land chord tones on strong beats — see and hear the target notes and guide tones (3 & 7) over one chord or through changes.

Turn on Groove, then hit Play progression — the chords sustain as a bed and a synth drum kit locks to the same clock. Loop keeps the changes cycling so you can blow over them.
Root Guide tone (3 / 7) Other chord tone (5)
Improvise over a backing track and start & end phrases on a lit note, especially the amber guide tones (3rd & 7th), landed on beats 1 & 3. Hit Play progression and watch/hear the guide tones shift by a step or two between chords. That movement is the guide-tone line, the backbone of melodic soloing (Paul Davids L6.4; PMT Harmonic Analysis).
