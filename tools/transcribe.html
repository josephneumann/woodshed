Transcribe

Drop in a recording (or play live) and hear it turned into notes — polyphonic, chord-aware, runs on your machine via Basic Pitch.

Input
Mic level

Idle. The model (~900 KB) loads on first use.
Plays your take and lights notes up in time. The fretboard shows a plausible fingering — audio can't reveal which strings you actually used.
Tab A plausible fingering — not necessarily the strings/frets you used.

    
    

    

    
Polyphonic: it detects several notes at once, so it works on chords and full recordings, not just single notes. "Live" here means record → Stop → transcribe. Mic recording needs localhost or HTTPS.
