How to use it

Standard tuning is E A D G B E (low to high). Pluck one string and let it ring — pick a clean, sustained note, not a strum. The big letter is the nearest note; the needle shows how many cents you're off. Green zone is within ±5 cents. Blue letter = flat (tune up), amber = sharp (tune down). Tune up to the note when you can — it holds better. The reading is smoothed over the last few frames so it won't chatter.
